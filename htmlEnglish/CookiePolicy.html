BarBacoaBar Cookie Policy

What are cookies?

Cookies are small text files that are stored on your device (computer, mobile phone, tablet, etc.) when you visit a website. Cookies are essential for the functioning of the internet, providing numerous advantages in the provision of interactive services.

How do we use cookies?

At BarBacoaBar, we use cookies to enhance your browsing experience, gather statistics on the visits we receive, and personalize the content we show you.

Types of cookies

We use both first-party and third-party cookies. First-party cookies are those sent to your device from our own servers or domains. Third-party cookies are those sent to your device from a server or domain that is not managed by us.

How can you configure or disable your cookies?

You can allow, block, or delete the cookies installed on your device by configuring the settings of your internet browser. If you do not allow the installation of cookies in your browser, you may not be able to access some of our website's services.

Changes to the Cookie Policy

We may update the Cookie Policy of our website, so we recommend that you review this policy each time you access BarBacoaBar to be properly informed about how and for what purposes we use cookies.

Contact

If you have any questions about BarBacoaBar's Cookie Policy, you can contact us through our contact form.
